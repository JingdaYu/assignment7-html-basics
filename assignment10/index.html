Choosing a Starter

Your starter Pokémon is the first partner for your journey. In PokeMMO, a good starter can help you learn battle mechanics, type advantages, and early-game strategy.

Exploring Regions

PokeMMO lets players travel through different regions. Exploring towns, routes, gyms, and caves is part of the fun because each area gives you new challenges and goals.

Emphasis and Importance

Before entering a difficult battle, it is very important to check your team level and item supply. You should also pay attention to type matchups because they can make a battle much easier. If your team is not ready, spend a little more time training before challenging the gym leader.

Useful Items

Items that are useful before leaving town:

  • Poké Balls
  • Potions
  • Antidotes
  • Escape Rope

Gym Battle Prep

Steps for preparing for a gym battle:

  1. Check the gym leader's main type.
  2. Train Pokémon that have a type advantage.
  3. Buy healing items before entering the gym.
  4. Save your progress and start the battle.
